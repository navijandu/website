Better Together

OrderMyGear (OMG) is the industry leader in technology for the Team gear and promotional product space. We got there by investing in people who have the OMG clients' best interest at heart and seek to simplify their lives. 

We are building a team of OMG'ers who aren't afraid to push the boundaries of what's possible. Together, we work hard, collaborate constantly, lift one another up and challenge each other without fear. We also believe that a round of high-fives is a great way to end meetings.

Our people are the reason we’re trusted by industry leaders and mom-and-pop shops alike. For 4 years in a row, we have been recognized as one of the Inc. 5000 America's Fastest Growing Private Companies. In 2018, Tech Titans named us a Top Growth Company in Tech in North Dallas for the third year in a row.
